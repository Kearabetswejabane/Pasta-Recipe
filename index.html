Creamy Chicken Pasta Recipe

Creamy pasta is a type of pasta dish that features a rich and velvety sauce made from dairy products such as cream, milk, or cheese. These sauces create a luxurious texture and are often complemented by various ingredients like garlic, onions, herbs, and sometimes proteins such as chicken, shrimp, or bacon.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 30minutes
  • Preparatiion: 10minutes
  • Cooking: 20minutes

ingredients

  • Breasts: 2 boneless, skinless
  • Seasoning: salt, pepper
  • Cooking Oil: 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Pasta: 225g of your choice, Penne or spaghetti
  • Salt: For boiling Water
  • Butter : 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic: 2 Gloves,minced
  • Heavy Cream: 1 Cup
  • Chicken Broth: 1 Cup
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1 Cup,grated
  • Italian Seasoning: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt&Pepper: To Taste
